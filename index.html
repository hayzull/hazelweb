What is Year Up?

Year Up is a program that gives young adults an opportunity to become successful. This program combines hands-on skill development that will help prepare young adults to become successful in their professional careers and higher education.

What is the opportunity to divide?

The opporunity to divide is not charity, nor a handout. This is good sense. Economic justice for young adults equals economic prosperity for U.S. businesses, and the nation at large. .
